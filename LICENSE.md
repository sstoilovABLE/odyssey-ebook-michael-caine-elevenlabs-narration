This repository is a fork of the [Standard Ebooks edition of *The Odyssey* by Homer, translated by William Cullen Bryant](https://github.com/standardebooks/homer_the-odyssey_william-cullen-bryant). The fork author has (1) systematically substituted Roman character names used in Bryant's translation (Ulysses, Neptune, Minerva, Juno, Mercury, etc.) with their Greek equivalents (Odysseus, Poseidon, Athena, Hera, Hermes, etc.), so that the text aligns with the name conventions used throughout the ElevenLabs audiobook narrated by the AI voice of Sir Michael Caine (2026); (2) made small grammar and spelling fixes; (3) added an editor's note explaining the purpose of the ebook, and made miscellaneous other changes to match the Michael Caine narration. 

The fork author dedicates all modifications made in this repository to the worldwide public domain via the terms in the [CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).
