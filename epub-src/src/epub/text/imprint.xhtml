This is an unofficial, fan-made edition of William Cullen Bryant’s 1871 translation of the Odyssey, prepared as a reading companion to the audiobook narrated in the artificial voice of Sir Michael Caine (ElevenLabs, 2026). Throughout the text, the names of the gods and heroes have been restored to their Greek forms so that the printed page matches the names spoken in that narration.

It is a derivative work based on the Standard Ebooks edition of Bryant’s translation, which was produced by Standard Ebooks volunteers from public-domain page scans. This is not an official Standard Ebooks release and carries no endorsement from Standard Ebooks, which is not responsible for the name changes or any other alteration made in this edition.
